Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian dish that's a staple in every household.

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 350g mince beef
  • 2 cans of tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  1. Heat the oil in a frying pan, then cook the onions and garlic until lightly browned.
  2. Add the mince beef and cook until it turns brown.
  3. Add the cans of tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Cook the spaghetti according to the packet instructions.
  5. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the sauce and serve.
